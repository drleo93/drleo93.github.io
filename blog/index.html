The Engineer's Handbook of Machine Learning

Leonardo Pulga PhD
A practitioner-first guide to applied ML for engineering systems — from Gaussian processes and uncertainty quantification through deep time-series models, system identification, digital twin architecture, and safe reinforcement learning. Every chapter is self-contained: TL;DR, key equations, working code, callout boxes, and decision tables. Targeted at engineers who need to deploy models in safety-critical, resource-constrained, or regulated environments.
